1|# RoseBloX Privacy Policy
2|
3|**Effective Date:** February 14, 2026
4|**Last Updated:** February 14, 2026
5|
6|## Overview
7|
8|RoseBloX ("the App," "we," "our") is a creative coloring book mobile application designed for children. We are committed to protecting the privacy of every child and parent who uses our app. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app handles and how.
9|
10|## Information We Collect
11|
12|**We collect the MINIMUM information necessary to make the app work.** RoseBloX does NOT:
13|- Collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, photos of children, or any personally identifiable information (PII).
14|- Track location, contacts, or device identifiers used for advertising.
15|- Sell, rent, or share any data with third parties.
16|- Display third-party advertisements.
17|
18|**What is stored:**
19|- **On your device only (local storage):** the artist nickname you choose, your selected avatar emoji, your artwork drawings, your unlocked sticker packs, and your purchase history. This data never leaves your device unless you submit artwork to the public contest.
20|- **On our server (anonymous):** a randomly generated profile ID with no personal info attached, used solely to sync purchases and contest submissions.
21|
22|**Optional features that require permission:**
23|- **Camera & Photo Library:** Used ONLY when you choose the "AI Snap to Color" feature, to convert a photo you take into a coloring page. Photos are sent to the AI service for conversion and are NOT stored on our servers.
24|- **Internet access:** Required to download coloring pages, sticker packs, and contest entries.
25|
26|## Children's Privacy & COPPA Compliance
27|
28|RoseBloX is designed for children. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and Google Play's Designed for Families program:
29|- We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
30|- We do not enable behavioral advertising or interest-based ads.
31|- All in-app purchases are gated behind a parental math question.
32|- Contest submissions use only the nickname the artist chooses (which may be any name or pseudonym).
33|
34|## In-App Purchases
35|
36|All purchases inside the app are processed exclusively by Google Play Billing (on Android). RoseBloX never sees or stores your credit card or banking information.
37|
38|## Tip Jar (Voluntary Creator Support)
39|
40|If you choose to tip the creator via the in-app links (Cash App, Venmo, PayPal), you will be redirected to the respective external app. RoseBloX does not process or see any payment information sent through these external services.
41|
42|## AI-Powered Photo Conversion
43|
44|When you use the "AI Snap to Color" feature, the photo you select is temporarily transmitted to Google's Gemini AI service (via Emergent LLM infrastructure) for conversion into a coloring outline. The photo is:
45|- NOT stored on RoseBloX servers
46|- NOT used to train any AI model
47|- NOT shared with any other party
48|- Deleted from temporary processing memory after conversion
49|
50|## Data Retention & Deletion
51|
52|- Local data: stored only on your device. Uninstalling the app deletes it permanently.
53|- Server profile (anonymous): kept for 12 months from last activity, then automatically purged.
54|- To request immediate deletion of your anonymous profile, email **support@roseblox.app** with your profile ID (found in Settings).
55|
56|## Changes to This Policy
57|
58|We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted within the app and the Effective Date above will be updated.
